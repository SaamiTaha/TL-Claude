## Cannibalization Rules

1. **Each primary keyword appears on exactly one page** — no exceptions.
2. Supporting keywords may appear on their assigned page only.
3. If a supporting keyword overlaps with another page's primary, the primary page owns it.
4. Homepage owns the broadest terms; service pages own long-tail.
5. When writing copy, use the primary keyword in: H1, meta title, meta description, first paragraph, and at least one H2.
6. Supporting keywords go in body copy, H2s/H3s, alt text, and FAQ questions.

## Content Tone Rules

- Confident, authoritative, craft-focused — never salesy
- NEVER: "family business", "passion for landscaping", "we treat your yard like our own"
- Target: affluent homeowners + commercial property managers / HOAs
- Lead with expertise and results, not promises
